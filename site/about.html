About Us


Find out more about us and our company.

GameX! Games is a new video game publishing company located in Pune, India.
We are a team of passionate specialists with extensive knowledge about the video game industry. Our task is to make sure that players will be able to enjoy unique and unforgettable games. We are not just a game company - we are your game partner!.

Have you ever wanted to work in the video game industry? Join us!






Our history

GameX! Games was founded in 2020, starting as a determined group of enthusiastic gamers and experienced professionals. Our enthusiasm makes us understand players and our extensive knowledge about the video game industry helps us guide your game to market.





Our goals

Our goal is to support unique games and ambitious, skilled studios and to get wonderful games in front of players. We want to make great game ideas turn into reality and let everyone enjoy them. We care both about developers and communicating with players to give you the best experience—so don’t hesitate and go all in!
